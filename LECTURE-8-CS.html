Contextual Keywords: Contextual keywords have special meaning in certain contexts. In those contexts, they act as keywords. Outside those contexts, they can be used as identifiers. They are not technically reserved. We should consider the contextual keywords reserved and avoid using them for any other purpose. All contextual keywords are lower case.
