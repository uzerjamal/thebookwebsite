RULES FOR KEYWORDS IN C#:

·         All keywords are lowercase words

·         Keywords cannot be used an identifier i.e. keywords cannot be used  as a name of variable, method, class, interface, namespace, etc.

·         The same keywords cannot be used together in the same statement.

RULES FOR IDENTIFIERS:

·         Can be in upper case or lower case
·         Can be of any length
·         Can not begin with a  number
·         Underscore(_) is allowed
·         There should not be space between two word – which define identifier
·         The name may consist of letters, digits, and underscore
·         They must not begin with a digit
·         It should not a keyword
·         White space are not allowed
